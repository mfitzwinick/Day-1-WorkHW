A Recipe for Peace of Mind in Trying Times:

Music, Animals and a Sandwich

In these strange days, I've realized it's the little things that matter most in life. Whether I'm discovering great music on YouTube, or listening to a go-to playlist, I have found that music can help me to travel to places I've been or want to go to even if I'm really just walking around the block. Below are some of the artists I've listened to this past week:

  • H.E.R., born Gabriella Wilson, came on the music scene in 2009, and surely has a long career ahead
  • LSD, Sia's collaboration with British rapper, Labryinth, and American producer Diplo
  • SOAK's second album, "Grim Town", features "Knock Me Off My Feet" which although new, takes me back in time to simpler days
  • TRISHES marries a wonderful mix of global beats with modern pop
  • And of course, Van Halen will rock on forever!
